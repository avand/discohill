About

Nestled in the Sierra Foothills, this reimagined barn is just about an hour away from two of Yosemite's entrances. Subtle yet vibrant home decor, a well equipped kitchen, strong WiFi, a dedicated workstation, and comfortable living room, make this place the perfect place to stay when visiting the area by yourself or with a partner.
The space
The barn is the 2nd building on the property. The two-story space is totally private for your use but please be mindful that you may see other guests during your stay. The barn has an open kitchen and living room downstairs, as well as a bathroom with large bathtub. Upstairs, you'll find another bathroom with a shower, a queen bed, and a workspace.
Guest access
You'll have the 1-bedroom barn exclusively to yourself. The outdoor kitchen and BBQ are for use of the guests of the neighboring building. Please respect their privacy and use of that space. The hot tub is a shared amenity and we encourage guests to make new friends, share travel tips, and be respectful of one another's privacy.
Other things to note
We keep the barn squeaky clean and we disinfect between every guest. Please keep our community safe by complying with local county and state policies. Don't forget to pack your patience and sense of adventure. Living in the woods has its pitfalls from time to time: pests, power outages, and inclement weather, to name a few. We strive for every guest to have a perfect stay and are prepared for almost anything. Still, things do come up and we thank you in advance for your understanding.
