About

About 1 hr from Yosemite and 20 min from downtown Mariposa, this cabin offers a place to relax and unwind. Exclusive access to the entire cabin, outdoor kitchen, and BBQ. “The stars from the jacuzzi were the best I’ve ever seen” - Leslie “Definitely one of the best Airbnbs out there” - Dulce “Possibly the comfiest bed I’ve slept in” - Alexis & Riley “Outdoor kitchen, hot tub, stars… an absolute dream!” - Delaram
The space
The cabin is fully remodeled with modern stainless steel appliances, propane-powered range, and granite counters. Heating and AC is at the ready, as well as high speed internet (25Mbps; cell service is spotty but WiFi calling works great!). Behind the house is an outdoor kitchen equipped with a BBQ, hot tub, and disco ball! On a clear night, you'll have an incredible view of the stars. 420 friendly but outside only, please.
Guest access
You'll have the 1-bedroom cabin exclusively to yourself. The outdoor kitchen and BBQ is also exclusively for use of cabin guests. The hot tub is a shared amenity with guests in our neighboring Airbnb, however, it's rare that guests cross paths and, when they do, embrace it as an opportunity to meet fellow travelers, share tips about their travels, and make new friends.
Other things to note
We keep the cabin squeaky clean and we disinfect between every guest. Please keep our community safe by complying with local county and state policies. Don't forget to pack your patience and sense of adventure. Living in the woods has its pitfalls from time to time: pests, power outages, and inclement weather, to name a few. We strive for every guest to have a perfect stay and are prepared for almost anything. Still, things do come up and we thank you in advance for your understanding.
